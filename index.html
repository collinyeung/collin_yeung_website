Collin is a mixed media artist from Toronto, Ontario who specializes in photo and video editing as well as digital drawing. Taking inspiration from the realms of contemporary fashion, art, photography, and film, his creations embody a unique fusion of diverse influences. With this blend of various elements, he crafts visually stunning works of art.

Currently enrolled in the Global Business and Digital Arts program at the University of Waterloo, Collin Yeung is honing his skills between creativity, technology, and business.

Collin's art pushes beyond conventional boundaries, inviting viewers into a world where the worlds of digital and traditional mediums collide.
